Maths In Nature

Maths is all around us

Maths is all around us- everywhere we look. Not just in the classroom, but out there in the big wide world. From the Golden Ratio in snails shells to the perfect symmetry of butterfly wings, the probability of finding a four-leaf clover to a peacock's feathers. Have you ever stopped and thought about just how and why such wonders exist?
Well, Maths in Nature is here to inspire and educate about the maths that surrounds us.
